Wano Country

Wano Country is a country in the New World that is not affiliated with the World Government. It is located on the north
side of the New World, northwest to Egghead, southwest to Winner Island, and west to Elbaf

Marine Headquarters

The Marine Headquarters is the main division of the Marine organization that has jurisdiction over the Grand Line.
It is one of the Three Great Powers in the world, alongside the Four Emperors
and the Seven Warlords of the Sea, who were later replaced by the SSG.

Whole Cake Island

Whole Cake Island is a New World island under the control of Big Mom,
formerly one of the Four Emperors. It is her crew's base of operations and the central island in
Big Mom's 35-island archipelago known as Totto Land.

Dressrosa

Dressrosa is an island and kingdom within the New World,
and one of the twenty kingdoms that founded the World Government. It was first mentioned by Vergo.
Dressrosa is the second island visited by the Straw Hat Pirates in the New World and the primary setting of the Dressrosa Arc.

Momoiro Island

Momoiro Island is an island in Paradise. It is located close to the Lulusia Kingdom.
The island is filled with pink animals and plants. It is home to the Kamabakka Kingdom , a place where "those with a heart of a maiden" gather.
